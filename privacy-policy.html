Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

PawGuide ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from visitors ("you"), how we use and safeguard that information, and what rights you have regarding your data when you visit our website. By accessing or using our Website, you consent to the data practices described herein.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Voluntarily Provided Data: Your name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, submit a contact form, or leave a comment. This information is provided voluntarily and is never required to browse our Website.
  • Automatically Collected Technical Data: Our servers and third-party analytics tools may automatically record your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution, language preference, referring URL, pages visited, click behavior, and time spent on each page.
  • Cookie and Tracking Data: We use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze usage, remember preferences, and deliver relevant advertising. See the "Cookies" section below.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Website and its content.
  • To deliver newsletters and pet care updates you have opted into.
  • To analyze visitor behavior using aggregated, anonymized data to improve our content.
  • To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense and other ad networks.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent security incidents or fraud.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

Google AdSense & Personalized Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its certified partners use cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on your browsing history on our Website and across the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, the NAI Opt-Out Page, or the DAA Opt-Out.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to analyze traffic patterns. This service collects aggregated data about page views, session duration, traffic sources, and visitor demographics. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core site functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized data collection on visitor behavior.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by ad networks to deliver relevant ads.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personally identifiable information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with analytics and advertising partners. Third-party services on our Website operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law. Newsletter data is kept until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained in anonymized form.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS/TLS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no electronic storage or transmission method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute data security.

Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately for deletion.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws (GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, etc.), you may have these rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing for direct marketing.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time.
  • Non-Discrimination: Exercise rights without discriminatory treatment (CCPA).

To exercise these rights, contact us below. We will respond within 30 days.

International Data Transfers

By using our Website, you consent to the transfer of information to servers that may be located in jurisdictions with different data protection standards than your country of residence.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance. We encourage periodic review of this page.
